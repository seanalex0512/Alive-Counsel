Who We Are

About Alive Counsel

"Supporting people and organisations grow with confidence, clarity, and purpose."

Dr. Janice Tan is the founder and principal of Alive Counsel — a licensed counsellor, strength-based coach, and trainer. With over 20 years of experience in counselling, coaching, and education, she is committed to helping individuals and organisations grow, heal, and achieve lasting fulfilment.

Before stepping into the counselling and coaching space, Dr. Janice held leadership roles in the corporate financial sector. Her business background gives her a practical, well-rounded perspective — especially when working with professionals navigating high-pressure environments.

Amongst her key corporate clients are Intel, Keysight, D'home Mental Persatuan, Hanson Cement, Affin Group, Global Mosaic International, Bible College Malaysia, and Safe Harbour.
